ABOUT THE APP


This application was created by Team Express as part of University Bootcamp. Throughout the design we have had pet owners in mind, as we know how important they are to you & your family.


Our priority was allowing users to create a secure account, to store important information about their beloved four-legged friends, with plans to add more features in the future.


This application is currently a prototype so should you have any feedback or suggestions on the design or the functionality, you can email us here.


If you are interested in our code or other information about this project then you can view our Github repository or our Heroku.
